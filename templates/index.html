Welcome to Feel the Fern

We aim to address the problem of deteriorating air quality levels within the house and outdoors in both urban and rural areas.

Dense, low maintenance forests with high-oxygen yielding plants and intelligently crafted pots are constucted sustainably, using the open spaces in the house.

This can easily be accomodated right from small studio apartment to large farmhouses.
